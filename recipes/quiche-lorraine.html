Quiche Lorraine

Category: Main Course

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 3-4 tbsp ice water
  • 250g (about 8 oz) bacon, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Gruyere or Swiss cheese
  • Salty pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the crust: Pulse flour and salt in a food processor. Add cold butter and pulse until crumbly. Add ice water one tablespoon at a time until dough forms. Press into a 9-inch pie dish and chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Blind bake the crust with weights for 10-15 minutes, then remove weights and bake for 5 more minutes.
  3. Sauté the bacon in a pan until crisp. Remove bacon, keeping some fat in the pan.
  4. Sauté the diced onion in the bacon fat until translucent.
  5. Whisk eggs, heavy cream, grated cheese, and nutmeg together. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Spread the cooked bacon and onions across the bottom of the baked crust.
  7. Pour the egg mixture over the filling.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until the center is set and the top is golden brown.
  9. Let cool slightly before slicing.
