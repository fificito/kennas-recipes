Quiche Lorraine

Main Course

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Box of NESTLÉ® Media Crema
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Onion
  • 1/2 Teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 Cup Bacon diced into small squares
  • Puff pastry (for the base)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Sauté the bacon and remove it before it becomes crispy. Set aside.
  3. For the quiche, place the extended puff pastry over the base of a greased mold.
  4. Mix the eggs, NESTLÉ® Media Crema, nutmeg, salt, onion and stir in the bacon.
  5. Pour over the puff pastry and bake for 30 minutes or until puffed and golden.
  6. Let it cool on a rack for 20 minutes and serve.
