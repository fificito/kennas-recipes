Duck with Orange and Capsicum

View Original Source File

Category: Main Course

Ingredients

For 6 People:

  • 1 duck
  • 5 oranges
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the gastrique: Create a caramel with 200g of granulated sugar and three tablespoons of wine vinegar.
  2. Add citrus: Once the mixture has caramelized, stir in the juice of two oranges.
  3. Prepare the pan: Line an oven-proof dish with orange slices and place the duck on top.
  4. Roast: Drizzle the caramelized mixture over the duck, season lightly with salt and pepper, and place in the oven at 180°C (350°F), basting regularly.
  5. Finish: The duck should take on a dark red hue by the end of the cooking process.
  6. Note: Be careful not to let the oranges over-caramelize.

Cooking Time

3-4 hours
