Lemon Meringue Pie

Category: Desserts

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1 and 1/3 cups water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 Tablespoon lemon zest
  • 2 Tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

For the Meringue:

  • 5 large egg whites, room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

For the Crust:

  • 9-inch pie crust, blind baked

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and partially blind bake a 9-inch pie crust.
  2. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F (177°C).
  3. Whisk the egg yolks together in a medium bowl. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, whisk together water, granulated sugar, cornstarch, salt, lemon juice, and lemon zest until the mixture thickens and bubbles. Reduce heat to low.
  4. Slowly stream a few spoonfuls of the warm lemon mixture into the egg yolks to temper them. Whisk the egg yolk mixture back into the saucepan. Return to medium heat and cook until thick and bubbling. Remove from heat and whisk in the butter.
  5. Spread the warm filling into the partially baked crust.
  6. Beat egg whites and cream of tartar on medium speed for 1 minute, then high speed until soft peaks form. Add sugar and salt, and beat on high until glossy stiff peaks form. Spread the meringue over the filling, touching the crust edges.
  7. Bake on the lowest oven rack for 20-25 minutes. Cool at room temperature for 1 hour, then chill in the refrigerator for 4 hours before serving.
