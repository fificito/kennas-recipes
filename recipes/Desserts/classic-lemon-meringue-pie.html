Classic Lemon Meringue Pie

Original Source

Category: Desserts

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust:

  • 2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, cut into ¼” cubes and chilled
  • ¼ cup ice water

For the Lemon Curd:

  • 8 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ⅓ cup cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon zest
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature

For the Swiss Meringue:

  • 4 1⁄2 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 8 large egg whites

Instructions

  1. Make the pie crust: Combine flour and salt. Mix in butter until pea-size lumps form. Add ice water until dough comes together. Wrap and chill for at least 2 hours.
  2. Shape and Blind Bake: Roll crust to ⅛ inch thickness. Press into a 9-inch dish. Chill for 1 hour. Bake at 375°F with weights for 25-30 mins, then 10 more mins without weights. Cool on rack.
  3. Make the lemon curd: Whisk sugar, salt, cornstarch, zest, juice, and 2 cups water in a saucepan. Heat until thickened. Temper in beaten egg yolks slowly, then return to heat until bubbling. Whisk in butter and strain. Spread into baked crust.
  4. Make the Swiss meringue: Simmer water in a pot. Whisk confectioners' sugar and egg whites in a heat-proof bowl over the water until sugar dissolves (113°F). Beat on high speed until glossy stiff peaks form.
  5. Assemble and Bake: Top the warm curd with meringue. Bake at 400°F for about 8 minutes until peaks are light golden brown.
  6. Cool: Cool at room temperature for 1 hour, then chill in the refrigerator for 4 hours before serving.
